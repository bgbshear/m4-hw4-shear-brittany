Eva Noblezada

About

Eva is an American actress and singer best known for her roles as Kim in the 2014 revival of "Miss Saigon" and Eurydice in "Hadestown."

Early Life

Born in San Diego, California, in 1996, Noblezada moved to Charlotte, North Carolina, and attended the Northwest School of the Arts until she was 17.

Career

In 2013, Noblezada was one of five finalists for the National High School Musical Awards. Her performance during the awards ceremony caught the attention of casting director Tara Rubin, who arranged an audition for 17-year-old Noblezada in front of director Cameron Mackintosh, who was casting for the forthcoming West End revival of "Miss Saigon." Noblezada was cast as Kim, the lead role, and left school to star in the production in London. In 2018, she opened the lead role of Eurydice in "Hadestown" and performed in the show until her final performance on August 13, 2023.

Upcoming Projects

The Great Gatsby

Noblezada was most recently cast as Daisy in a new musical adaptation of "The Great Gatsby," which opened at the Paper Mill Playhouse on October 12, 2023. Noblezada stars across Jeremy Jordan, who plays Gatsby. The production received mostly positive reviews and is rumored to be headed to Broadway.
